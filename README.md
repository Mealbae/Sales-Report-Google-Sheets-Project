# Sales Performance Tracker & Data Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales and profit data for November 2024 to uncover actionable business insights. The analysis breaks down performance metrics across multiple dimensions, including product categories, regional sales, individual salesperson performance, and preferred payment methods. 

This repository contains the final analytical report and visualizations, demonstrating core data analyst skills in data aggregation, KPI tracking, and performance reporting.

## 🎯 Key Objectives
* **Track Revenue & Profitability:** Calculate and visualize total sales and profit margins for the month.
* **Identify Top Performers:** Evaluate sales representatives to determine the highest revenue generators.
* **Analyze Product Demand:** Assess the sales volume and revenue of various product lines to identify best-sellers.
* **Understand Customer Behavior:** Map out the most popular payment methods and regional sales distributions.

## 💡 Key Insights & Findings
Based on the November 2024 data analysis, the following key performance indicators (KPIs) were identified:
* **Overall Financial Performance:** The total sales for the month reached **$668,271**, generating a total profit of **$198,631**.
* **Top Performing Salesperson:** **Anna** led the sales team with the highest contribution of **$80,926**.
* **Product Performance:** **Product A** was the leading revenue driver, generating **$181,422**, followed closely by **Product C** at **$161,730**.
* **Payment Preferences:** The analysis tracked transactions across three primary payment methods: **Cash**, **Credit Card**, and **Online Payment**, with a significant portion of sales ($211,854) attributed to a single segment.

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset contains synthetic transactional records for November 2024, detailing individual sales. **The raw synthetic dataset used for this analysis is included directly in this repository.**

Key columns analyzed include:
* `Date`
* `Product` (A, B, C, D)
* `Sales Quantity` & `Sales Price`
* `Total Sales` & `Profit`
* `State` & `Salesperson`
* `Payment Method`

**Access the Data:** You can view the complete dataset directly via Google Sheets here: [Sales Data Spreadsheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qdAG-4K53FgVoJ7tSgEMcjqxjrTIYLvLO-BLyKR97dU/edit?usp=sharing)
